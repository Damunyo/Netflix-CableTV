Causal-Comparative study between the satisfaction of Netflix users and Cable TV users of Las Pinas City

Goal and Beneficiaries Statement of the Problem Scope and Delimitation
The goal of this research is to find if there is a significant difference between the levels of satisfaction between the users of Netflix and Cable TV.
The researchers believe that Television companies, Companies who plan to start an online streaming platform, and people who are unsure if they should get a cable tv or a Netflix subscription would most profit from the research conducted as it will inform them of what the pros and cons are when people choose Netflix over cable tv and vice versa.		 Specific:
1. How satisfied are the respondents with Netflix?
2. How satisfied are the respondents with Cable TV?
3. What are the possible factors for their satisfaction?

General:
1. Is there a significant difference between the level of satisfaction between Netflix and Cable TV users?

 This study will focus on the level of satisfaction of the respondents between the Netflix and Cable TV. The respondents of this study are the ones who use both Netflix and Cable TV only. This study will be conducted within Las Pinas City only.
